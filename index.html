About Us

E-Cell VSSUT, after having a great number of achievements, having organised E-Summits, Leadership summit, Hackathons, TEDx - all successsfully along with having startups ranging from emerging startups to successfully running startups. We understand the importance of coming together for a greater cause and the amount of exposure we can have after that. Fraternizing of similar organisations and strong collaboration with the motive of coming up with something really big is what E.A.S.T aims for Entrepreneurship Accords and Startup growth Traffic (E.A.S.T) is one of it's kind programme initiated by E-Cell, VSSUT. It encourages the entrepreneurship spirit and instils the idea of establishing similar organisations along with equating with the existing ones. We will be really glad on partenering with similar instituitions and we look forward for such collaborations.

Our Missions and Objectives

To promote entrepreneurial spirits.

Promote and spread startup culture nationwide.

Work to make the most of the resources available.

Establishment with highly efficient entrepreneurial mindsets

To outshine and prosper with competitive spirit.

To lead forward with unison withholding a solid foundation

Why Choose E.A.S.T


Network Expansion

An initiative can be taken up to create a network of all existing startups in our institutions with a mission to create and support aspiring entrepreneurs and drive entrepreneurship as a catalyst for job-creation and overall economic growth.

Social Media Activity

All the activities that are going on in different E-Cells under E.A.S.T would be made visible in all institutions, being a Mutual Campus Ambassador in each other's events.

Mutual Sharing of Resources

Resources like Datasheets, Developers, Designers and contacts everything will be shared among the partnered E-cells mutually.

Promotion, advertising and participation

All the events that are going to be organised in near future will have a proper visibility among all the institutions partnered under EAST. From promoting each other's events to taking part actively in all open events, participation and encouragement will be made the priority. To make events more successful they will have a good outreach from your side and this would remain mutual.

Internship opportunities would be shared

All the opportunities of Internship will be provided to the members of the programme through various internship platforms existing among all the E-Cells under EAST.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EAST and Why EAST?
EAST (Entrepreneurship Accords and Startup growth Traffic) is a network building programme by E-Cell, VSSUT which aims to collaborate with similar organisations and to foster entrepreneurship. EAST can be a great platform where various E-cells can come together and share their resources and enhance their network for mutual benefits.
How long will the collaboration last?
The collaboration will last for six months for minimum and further, it will be depending upon the MoU agreement.
Is there any monetary policy?
No. There is no monetary policy, we believe in working on mutual cooperation and interest.
Up to what stage will EAST help different student entrepreneurs and their startups to grow?
As the aim suggests, we work on enhancing contacts which can further be used as an opportunity in developing and upholding of any startup. EAST can provide opportunities sharing platform to the startups.
How to be a member of the programme EAST?
For having the membership in EAST you just have to fill in basic details on the website through Typeform available in the website and further proceedings will be carried out by our admins.
What makes an E-cell qualified/unqualified for being a part of EAST or do(s) and don’t(s)?
The only criteria for any E-Cell is just to be a registered entrepreneurship society of their institutes. For do(s) and don't(s) part, you have to abide by all the terms and conditions of MoU and anything against that would lead to the banishment of that E-Cell from the programme.
Can you help us to set up an E-cell at our college? (If not already).
Yes. Encouraging entrepreneurial spirits among the colleges is what we aim for. So we can assist to form an E-Cell in your college from scratch if not already existing.
Any contract or agreement we have to sign beforehand?
Yes. To maintain some common grounds on any action to be taken by the programme, we shall sign a Memorandum of Understand (MoU).
